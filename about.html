About Us

Cake Beat is operated by Raman, a passionate baker based in Brampton, ON. Cake Beat is about premium quality custom made cakes, cupcakes and other baking delicacies. Spreading bliss and commending each snapshot of life is the mantra at Cake Beat . Cake Beat started their endeavor with Handmade Chocolates and gradually moved into Cakes, Pastries and unified items. Everything started with taking up little gathering requests. With the progression of time, we moved to counters in markets and now we are selling across GTA. Cake Beat develops with each passing day and connecting with individuals and adding to a mind-blowing festivals.
